💡 Design Mind

01 Empathy

I started by listening. I interviewed Sara to understand her love for cooking, her family stories, and why red and gold mean so much to her.

02 Define

I defined the real need: "Sara needs a way to share her traditional recipes and family stories to preserve her cultural heritage."

03 Ideate

I brainstormed colors, layouts, and content — red & gold, recipe lists, family photos, and a personal story section.

04 Prototype

I built a simple version with HTML & CSS. It wasn't perfect, but it was real. I could see what worked and what didn't.

05 Test

I showed it to others. Feedback made it better: more stories, better images, clearer recipes. Iteration is everything.
